Laura Roman-Gonzalez holds a Bachelor’s degree in Biotechnology from Interamerican University of Puerto Rico and a second Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Old Dominion University. She is currently pursuing a Master’s in Data Analytics. Laura's professional journey began in a Biotechnology Research Laboratory, where she developed a strong foundation in scientific research. After graduating, she transitioned to an Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) Assistant role, followed by a position as a Process Improver in the medical device manufacturing sector. Her career progressed as she took on a role in Quality Control, inspecting finished products. In 2019, while completing her second degree, Laura became a Quality Engineer at an aerospace company, later moving into the automotive industry as a Supplier Quality Engineer. Currently, she works as a CAPA Engineer in assembly manufacturing for Home and Security products, where she focuses on continuous improvement and quality assurance.
